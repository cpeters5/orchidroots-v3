Upload Instruction

Upload from Web
  • You must be the owner of the photo you wish to upload.
  • Make sure you have the correct identity of hte the orchid in the photo
  • Search for the orchid in OrchidRoots and navigate to its detail page
  • Click on the "Access Area" button in the upper left hand corner of the page and select "My Photo Page"
  • Click on "Upload local file" button to start an uppload form
  • Select the author name from the dropdown menu. If your name does not exist, cancel upload and notify admin to register asd a donor.
  • Click on "Choose File" button and select the image file you wish to upload
  • Fill in the rest of the form as appropriate
  • Add your comment. At the end of hte comment, enter the name of the author of the comment, or you own name if the comment is made by you.
  • Click "Save" button to upload, or the "Cancel" link to discard and go back to Identification page.
